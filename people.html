  • Dr. Yanyan Zhang张岩岩

    Assistant Professor

    • Postdoc Researcher, Environmental Engineering, McGill University, Canada (2016-2020)
    • Ph.D., Environmental Geography, Peking University, China (2010-2015)
    • Visiting Student, Environmental Chemistry, the CAES, U.S. (2013-2014)
    • B.S., Environmental Science, Wuhan University, China (2006-2010)

    Dr. Zhang joined Westlake University in February 2021 and established the Degradation and Analysis of Emerging Contaminants Lab. Her research combines experimental investigations and theoretical calculations to elucidate the degradation mechanisms of fluorochemicals, particularly per and polyfluoroalkyl substances, to develop innovative materials and processes for water treatment and site remediation, and to characterize the occurrence and environmental fate of hidden fluorochemicals. Dr. Zhang is the recipient of the 2026 James J. Morgan ES&T Early Career Award from the American Chemical Society and the 2025 Young Environmental Chemist Award from the Chinese Chemical Society. She currently serves as the Associated Editor for npj Emerging Contaminants and a member for ES&T Early Career Editorial Advisory Board. Outside the lab, she loves hiking, planting, and gardening.

    Google Scholar

  • Dr. Xinyu Guo 郭心雨

    Postdoctoral Researcher

    • Ph.D., Plant Nutrition, Zhejiang University (2017-2022)
    • B.E., English, Huazhong University of Science & Technology (2015-2017)
    • B.E., Agricultural Resources and Environment, Huazhong Agricultural University (2013-2017)

    Dr. Xinyu Guo is a joint Postdoctoral Researcher under the supervision of Dr. Yanyan Zhang and Dr. Lei Wang. She joined Westlake University in May 2023. She works on the absorption, accumulation, and transformation of PFASs in plants. She used to focus on the mechanism of cell wall modification on heavy metal uptake and accumulation in plants. Outside the lab, she loves playing the piano and hiking.
